Calming Cleanser

Oat and allantoin, no fragrance at all; reactive skin cleansed without comment.

A fragrance-free gel-cream cleanser for reactive skin that removes impurities. For face and body.

Dosage
One pump for the face; two to three for the body.
  • Oat is recognised for a characteristic constituent set: a systematic review of 154 studies catalogued 113 bioactive compounds reported in the grain, and established that beta-glucans are significantly higher in oat than in buckwheat, with avenanthramides and steroidal saponins characteristically present.1
  • The inner leaf gel of Aloe vera is recognised for carrying the majority of the plant's reported bioactive compounds — acetylated polysaccharides, with phenolic acids and flavonoids quantified alongside them.3
  • Aloe leaf juice has been used in cosmetic preparation for a very long time; the dermatological review that records this use states plainly that controlled trials are still required to determine real efficacy, and we repeat that here rather than around it.4

The properties above are drawn from the published literature cited below. They describe the constituents of the ingredients, not a tested effect of this product.

Principal constituents

Coco-Glucoside
The surfactant. An alkyl polyglucoside; it does the cleansing.
Avena Sativa Kernel Extract
Oat kernel. The grain's reported constituents include beta-glucans, avenanthramides and steroidal saponins; quantities in the literature are ranges, not fixed values, because extraction methods differ across studies.
Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice
Inner leaf gel — the polysaccharide fraction, principally acetylated mannans. Distinct from the leaf latex, which is a different material and is not what is used here.
Glycerin
Humectant.
Allantoin
A single defined molecule, used at low concentration.
Panthenol
Provitamin B5.
Sodium Benzoate, Potassium Sorbate
The preservative system. Citric acid sets the pH at which it works.

Full ingredients — INCI

Aqua, Coco-Glucoside, Glycerin, Avena Sativa Kernel Extract, Allantoin, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Panthenol, Sodium Benzoate, Potassium Sorbate, Citric Acid.

Vessel
200 mL pump bottle.
Recyclability
The pump assembly is a mixed-material component with a metal spring and does not recycle with the bottle; separate it before disposal.

Storage. Closed, away from direct light and heat. Rinse-off, water-based and preserved; use within a reasonable period of opening.

Precautions. External use. Avoid the eyes. Oat is a cereal: contact sensitisation to oat-derived cosmetic materials is documented, and anyone with a known oat or cereal sensitivity should treat this accordingly. Patch test on the inner forearm before first use. Nothing on this page is a claim to treat any condition — the literature cited establishes what is in these materials, not what they do to skin.
