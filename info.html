Practical matters

The house is small and speaks plainly. What follows is what we can promise today; anything we cannot yet promise is marked as such.

Shipping & returns

Dispatch

Adama is taking advance orders. Each formulation ships when its first production run is bottled and released — orders are dispatched in the sequence they were received, and you will be written to when yours leaves.

Shipping

Shipping is complimentary on orders over $75. Below that, the exact carrier rate is calculated at checkout — we do not mark it up.

Returns

An unopened vessel may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Opened formulations cannot be resold and cannot be returned, except where the product is faulty — in which case we replace or refund it, whichever you prefer.

The full carrier list and international duties table will be published here when the store opens.

Questions, asked often

When will my advance order arrive?

When the first run of that formulation is finished. Small-batch stoneware and cold-pressed oils move at their own pace; we will not hurry either. Your order email carries the current estimate.

Are the formulations vegan?

All are plant- and mineral-derived except the Shikakai & Honey Shampoo, which carries honey. Nothing is tested on animals, by us or on our behalf.

Why is there no label on the vessel?

The stoneware is engraved rather than labelled — brand, formulation and its signature botanical are fired into the clay. Net contents, the full INCI list, usage and precautions are printed on the recyclable carton and the enclosed leaflet, and published in full on every product page.

What do I do with the empty vessel?

Keep it. Fired stoneware is made to outlive its contents — rinse it and refill it, or let it hold something else entirely. The dispensing fittings (pump, dropper, atomiser) are mixed-material parts and go to general waste.

Should I patch test?

Yes — with any new formulation, ours included. Apply a little to the inner forearm and wait a day. Every product page lists declarable allergens under its full INCI.

Where are the formulations made?

Formulated and filled in small batches; each ingredient's origin is named on its product page — Chilean rosehip, Moroccan argan and ghassoul, Ghanaian shea, Icelandic volcanic sand.
