Terms of sale.

Advance orders, in plain language: what you agree to, what we promise, and what happens if we are late.

Advance orders

Everything sold here today is sold as an advance order: each formulation ships when its first production run is bottled. You are charged when you place the order, and your place in the queue is dated from that moment. When your order leaves, you will be written to with tracking.

If a run is delayed

Small-batch stoneware and cold-pressed oils are slow by nature. If your order's estimated dispatch moves by more than thirty days, we will write to you with the new estimate and the offer of an immediate full refund.

Cancellation

You may cancel any unshipped advance order at any time, for any reason, for a full refund — one email is enough.

Prices & payment

All prices are in US dollars and include the vessel — the stoneware is the packaging, and it is yours to keep. Taxes and shipping are calculated at checkout; shipping is complimentary over $75.

Returns & faults

An unopened vessel may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Opened formulations cannot be resold and cannot be returned — except where the product is faulty, in which case we replace or refund it, whichever you prefer.

The seller

Adama is in its founding season. The operating entity, registered address and responsible-person details will be published here before the first order is charged — no order will be taken before they are.

Contact

Questions about these terms: hello@adama.earth.
