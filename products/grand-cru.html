Dosage
Three to four drops.
  • Prickly pear seed oil is among the most linoleic-acid-dominant of plant oils: analyses of Moroccan seed oil report linoleic acid at 57–64% of the fatty acids, with oleic acid at 14–19%.1
  • The same oil carries tocopherols in the range of 500–680 mg/kg, gamma-tocopherol predominant, and phytosterols at 8,000–11,100 mg/kg, of which beta-sitosterol is the largest single sterol. Figures vary with the provenance of the seed.1
  • A separate analysis identifies tyrosol, vanillic acid, vanillin, ferulic acid, pinoresinol and cinnamic acid among the phenolic constituents of prickly pear seed oil.2
  • Rose hip oil is reported to carry phytosterols, tocopherols and carotenoids, together with phenolic compounds — predominantly p-coumaric acid methyl ester.3
  • Jojoba is not an oil in the ordinary sense. Its seed reserve is liquid wax ester — long-chain fatty acids esterified to long-chain fatty alcohols — rather than the triacylglycerols that make up the other oils here.4
  • The acid moieties of the jojoba esters are principally eicosenoic, erucic and oleic; tocopherols and phytosterols accompany them.5

The properties above are drawn from the published literature cited below. They describe the constituents of the ingredients, not a tested effect of this product.

Principal constituents

Prickly pear seed oil (Opuntia ficus-indica, seed)
Linoleic-acid-dominant; gamma-tocopherol the predominant tocopherol, beta-sitosterol the predominant sterol. Reported values vary several-fold by provenance, so ranges are given rather than a figure.
Squalane
A saturated hydrocarbon carried as the diluent of the oil phase. No verified literature is held for this material; it therefore carries no property statement above.
Rose hip oil (Rosa canina, fruit)
Reported to carry tocopherols, phytosterols and carotenoids.
Immortelle (Helichrysum italicum, flower)
Carried as the flower essential oil. No verified literature is held for this material; it therefore carries no property statement above.
Jojoba wax esters (Simmondsia chinensis, seed)
Liquid wax ester, not a triglyceride. Simmondsia chinensis stands alone in its own family, Simmondsiaceae.
Tocopherol
Added to the oil phase against oxidation.

Full ingredients — INCI

Opuntia Ficus-Indica Seed Oil, Squalane, Rosa Canina Fruit Oil, Helichrysum Italicum Flower Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil, Tocopherol, Parfum, Limonene, Linalool.

Declarable fragrance allergens

Limonene, Linalool

Vessel
Dropper bottle, 30 mL, with collar and bulb.
Material
Stoneware body; the dropper assembly is a separate part.
Recyclability
The stoneware is fired clay — kept and refilled rather than recycled. Separate the dropper from the bottle before disposal — the assembly is a mixed part.

Storage. Keep closed, upright, away from light and heat. Plant oils oxidise; the opaque stoneware slows it, the cap does the rest.

Precautions. External use only. Avoid the eye area. Contains Helichrysum italicum flower oil, an essential oil of the Asteraceae family; those with a known sensitivity to that family should take care. Contains the declarable fragrance allergens limonene and linalool. Patch test before first use.
