Privacy, plainly.

The house collects as little as it can. This page says exactly what, and why — in one screen, without a single tracker behind it.

What we collect

Two things, and only when you give them. If you leave your email address for the letter, we keep that address for the sole purpose of writing to you. If you add products to your cart, that cart is stored in your own browser's local storage — it never leaves your device and we cannot see it.

What we do not do

There are no analytics scripts, no advertising pixels, and no third-party trackers on this site. We do not sell, rent or share your email address with anyone. We set no cookies.

When orders open

Placing an order will require the details any shop needs — name, delivery address, email — handled by our payment and fulfilment providers under their own published terms. We will name those providers here before the first order is taken.

Your rights

Write to us at any time to see what we hold about you (it will be your email address, at most) or to have it deleted. One message is enough; deletion is immediate and confirmed in reply.

Contact

For anything concerning your data: hello@adama.earth.
