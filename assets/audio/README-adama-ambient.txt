Title:  From the Earth

Style of Music:
Earthy ambient meditation, warm and organic. Slow, hypnotic hang drum and
kalimba over a deep resonant drone; soft fingerpicked nylon guitar, breathy low
bansuri flute, a gentle frame-drum heartbeat. Field recordings of desert wind
and trickling water woven underneath. Analog tape warmth, spacious cathedral
reverb, no beat — just a slow breathing pulse. Grounding, luxurious, still; the
feeling of a sunlit stone apothecary at dawn. ~60 BPM, D dorian, seamless loop.

Exclude styles:
vocals, lyrics, drums, drum machine, EDM, synth-pop, bright, poppy, aggressive,
cinematic swell, brass

(Compact / tag version if the style box is short:
 warm organic ambient, meditative, hang drum, kalimba, deep drone, nylon guitar,
 bansuri flute, soft frame drum, desert wind + water field recordings, tape warmth,
 spacious reverb, no beat, 60 bpm, D dorian, seamless loop, instrumental, calm)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Why this fits ADAMA:
- "From the earth, for the body" — raw-nature textures (wind, water, wood, stone).
- Aesop/apothecary register: calm, unhurried, editorial stillness — never a jingle.
- Palette-matched to the visuals: warm, mineral, meditative; supports browsing,
  doesn't compete with it.
Alt direction if you want it more Moroccan/hammam: swap bansuri for ney flute and
kalimba for a soft oud, keep everything else.
