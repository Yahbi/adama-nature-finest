Our philosophy

Adama is the Hebrew word for earth, and for the ground a thing grows in. The word for a human being shares its root.

Hebrew אֲדָמָה adamah, earth; אָדָם adam, a human being. Arabic took the name — آدَم, Ādam — and keeps the root in أَدِيم, adīm: the surface of the earth.

We formulate with plants and minerals of known provenance, publish every ingredient list in full, and sell ten of our raw materials unblended — so you can judge the compound against its parts.
