How to use
Smooth over damp skin after bathing, or layer beneath body cream. For external use.
  • Jojoba is not an oil in the ordinary sense. Its seed reserve is liquid wax ester — long-chain fatty acids esterified to long-chain fatty alcohols — rather than the triacylglycerols that make up the other three oils here.1
  • The acid moieties of the jojoba esters are principally eicosenoic, erucic and oleic; tocopherols and phytosterols accompany them.2
  • Jojoba wax has long been used in dermatological and cosmetic formulation, on the strength of that wax-ester structure.3
  • Rose hip oil is reported to carry phytosterols, tocopherols and carotenoids, together with phenolic compounds — predominantly p-coumaric acid methyl ester.4
  • Sunflower seed oil is reported to carry tocopherols, phytosterols and phenolic constituents alongside its triacylglycerol fraction.5

The properties above are drawn from the published literature cited below. They describe the constituents of the ingredients, not a tested effect of this product.

Principal constituents

Jojoba wax esters (Simmondsia chinensis, seed)
Liquid wax ester, not a triglyceride. Simmondsia chinensis stands alone in its own family, Simmondsiaceae.
Sweet almond oil (Prunus amygdalus dulcis, seed)
Carried here as a triacylglycerol oil. No verified literature is held for this material; it therefore carries no property statement above.
Rose hip oil (Rosa canina, fruit)
Reported to carry tocopherols, phytosterols and carotenoids.
Sunflower seed oil (Helianthus annuus, seed)
Reported to carry tocopherols and phytosterols. Fatty-acid balance varies by cultivar; no ratio is stated here.
Tocopherol
Added to the oil phase against oxidation.

Full ingredients — INCI

Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis Oil, Rosa Canina Fruit Oil, Helianthus Annuus Seed Oil, Tocopherol, Parfum, Limonene, Linalool.

Declarable fragrance allergens

Limonene, Linalool

Vessel
Cylindrical stoneware dropper bottle, 100 mL, with wood collar and dropper.
Material
Stoneware body; the dropper assembly is a separate part.
Recyclability
The stoneware is fired clay — kept and refilled rather than recycled. Separate the dropper from the bottle before disposal — the assembly is a mixed part.

Storage. Keep closed, upright, away from light and heat. Plant oils oxidise; the opaque stoneware slows it, the cap does the rest.

Precautions. External use only. Avoid the eye area. Contains sweet almond oil, a tree-nut derivative. Contains the declarable fragrance allergens limonene and linalool. Patch test before first use.
