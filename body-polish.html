Volcanic Sand Body Polish

$42 200 mL

Icelandic volcanic sand, milled fine as flour, with castor oil.

A mineral scrub of volcanic sand, with castor and plant oils.

Key ingredients
  • Volcanic Sand
  • Castor Oil
  • Cane Sugar
How to use
On damp skin in the shower, massage in circular motions, then rinse. One to two times weekly.
Suited to
Body.
How to use
On damp skin in the shower, work over the body in small circles, then rinse thoroughly.
Dosage
One to two times weekly.

Principal constituents

Sucrose
Crystalline sugar, and the bulk of the formula. It is water-soluble, so it dissolves as it is worked and leaves with the rinse.
Basaltic Mineral Sand
Milled basalt, an igneous silicate rock. It does not dissolve in water; it is the insoluble half of the abrasive fraction, and it leaves with the rinse rather than in it.
Ricinus Communis Seed Oil
Castor oil. With coconut oil it forms the continuous phase the two abrasives are suspended in.
Cocos Nucifera Oil
Coconut oil. The second component of the oil phase.
Citrus Aurantium Dulcis Peel Oil
Sweet orange peel oil. Its dominant volatile is d-limonene, which is why Limonene is declared separately at the end of the ingredient list.
Tocopherol
Carried in the oil phase, where it guards the oils against oxidation.
Sodium Benzoate
Preservative.

Full ingredients — INCI

Sucrose, Ricinus Communis Seed Oil, Basaltic Mineral Sand, Cocos Nucifera Oil, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis Peel Oil, Tocopherol, Sodium Benzoate, Limonene.

Declarable fragrance allergens

Limonene

Vessel
200 mL jar
Material
Stoneware body with a screw lid.
Recyclability
The stoneware is fired clay — rinse it out, keep it and refill. Separate the lid before disposal.

Storage. Keep the jar closed, away from direct sunlight and heat. Do not let water stand in the jar between uses: the sugar fraction is soluble and will dissolve.

Precautions. For external use. Avoid contact with the eyes. Do not use on broken, inflamed or sunburned skin. This is a mechanical abrasive; work it lightly and let the material do the work. Discontinue if irritation occurs. Keep out of reach of children.
